Biography

Xidao Wen is a Ph.D candidate at PITT Computational Social Science Lab (Picso), from the School of Computing and Information, University of Pittsburgh. His research interests include data mining, machine learning and Information Visualization. He is advised by Dr. Yu-Ru Lin.
